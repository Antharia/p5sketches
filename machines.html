Lines

Two lines, \(a_1x+by_1+\;c_1=0\) and \(a_2x+by_2+\;c_2=0\) intersect if \(a_1b_2\neq a_2b_1\)

The point of intersection is

$$x=(b_1c_2-b_2c_1)/(a_1b_2-a_2b_1)$$

$$y=(a_1c_2-a_2c_1)/(a_1b_2-a_2b_1)$$
