Lines

Two lines, a1x+by1+ c1=0 and a2x+by2+ c2=0 intersect if a1b2≠a2b1

The point of intersection is

x=(b1c2-b2c1)/(a1b2-a2b1)

y=(a1c2-a2c1)/(a1b2-a2b1)
